  • dry

What products to use for each skin type

As we know, each skin type has different needs, so you should learn what you need depending on you.

  • For oily skin, cleanse with an exfoliating cleanser morning and night. Use a toner that contains salicylic acid, glycolic acid, or lactic acid. Treat your skin with benzoyl peroxide or retinol. Moisturize morning and night with a lightweight moisturizer.
